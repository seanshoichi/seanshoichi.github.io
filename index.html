About

I've been shooting since 2011, based out of Livermore, CA.

My eye was shaped early on by the moody photographers I'd stumble across on Tumblr — the ones who seemed to find something alive in shadow and stillness. Dark lighting, quiet settings, a little mystery. That's always been home for me. Overly bright, overly polished imagery tends to feel like it's trying too hard — too loud to sit with.

What I'm drawn to in a photo is something beautiful but not quite comfortable — a little mysterious, almost fantasy-like, with a touch of somberness. I'm an introvert by nature, and I think that bleeds into the work whether I intend it to or not.

I'll occasionally try different styles and settings just to stretch a bit, but moody is where I always land. It's just the aesthetic that feels most like mine.
